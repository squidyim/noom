Welcome!

Record all your thoughts and dreams throughout life.


This is a site to keep track of all the thoughts you've had in life. To record thoughts click on the navbar up top and click on the section thoughts. In there you will see a space to write what you please! This can also be use in many other ways like a todo list or just keeping notes! This is a safe space of whatever you please!

My Mission

The type of users that I am trying to reach are people of all ages wanting to jot random thoughts or need to clear their mind. What I would hope for users to get from this page is a safe space for people to relieve stress and get off what's on their mind. There are many people out there who are burdenend from their own head so a problem I wanted to fix is for users to have something quick and easy and create that safe space for them.
